Frequently Asked Questions

How do you define "transphobic content"?

Our AI system analyzes tweets for language that denies trans identities, promotes harmful stereotypes, or contributes to discrimination. The system is trained on examples from trans rights organisations and reviewed by experts.

How does the AI system work?

Our AI monitors JK Rowling's tweets in real time and flags potential transphobic content for human review. You can see the AI analysis system in action and review past assessments at our analysis dashboard.

Can I cancel my pledge anytime?

Yes! You can cancel your pledge at any time by emailing us. No questions asked.

What if I reach my monthly limit?

Once you reach your monthly limit, no further charges will be made until the next month. You'll receive an email notification when you're close to your limit.

Do you take any money from the donations?

We take only the amount to cover card transactions and website costs. We minimise transaction costs by charging the transactions in bulk rather than as micro-transactions.

How do I know my money is going to the right place?

We provide detailed receipts and monthly reports showing exactly which organisations received your donations. All organisations are verified UK charities.
